    About us

    OSTP is wholly owned by the Mataya family.

    It was established in March 1997. Its core business being a retail and wholesale pharmaceutical company with a focus in selling medicaments and health related products.

    It currently has four branches in all the four regions of Malawi, thus central, south, north and eastern region of Malawi in the districts of Lilongwe, Blantyre, Mzuzu and Zomba. Blantyre being the head office of OSTP.
